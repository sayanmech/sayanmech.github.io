Research

Ongoing Research

I am currently conducting research at the School of Energy Science and Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, under the mentorship of Dr. Mayur Prakash Bonkile. My present research focuses on physics-based battery modelling, with emphasis on developing high-fidelity numerical frameworks for electrochemical and thermal behaviour prediction of battery systems.

My co-supervisor is Prof. Jeevanjyoti Chakrabarty from the Department of Mechanical Engineering, IIT Kharagpur. The research involves multiphysics modelling, simulation-driven design, and advanced computational approaches for next-generation energy storage systems.

Doctoral Research

I completed my doctoral research at the Heat Transfer and Thermal Power Laboratory, Department of Mechanical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Madras under the supervision of Prof. C. Balaji.

My doctoral work focused on electronic cooling and battery thermal management, addressing high heat flux thermal control challenges in modern electronic and energy systems. The research was primarily experimental in nature, complemented by targeted numerical modelling for validation, performance analysis, and design optimisation.

The work contributed to improved understanding of cyclic heat load behaviour, thermal mitigation strategies, and the development of advanced cooling architectures for electronics and battery systems.
