About Me

I am an Institute Post Doctoral Fellow at the School of Energy Science and Engineering, IIT Kharagpur. My research focuses on physics-based battery modelling, electrochemical systems, electronic cooling, and battery thermal management. My work integrates experimental and numerical approaches to develop advanced thermal solutions for next-generation energy systems.
